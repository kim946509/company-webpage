WOORI M-Tech

A Company Collaborating with Clients in the Global Environment

"Specialized in Injection Mold Manufacturing and Export"

WOORI M-Tech Co., Ltd. is a specialized company in plastic injection molds for visual appliances, home appliances, and automotive parts.

Since its establishment in 1990, we have consistently pursued high-quality and short delivery times for customer satisfaction. We continuously compete in mold technology development, equipment investment, mold quality improvement, and mass production quality stabilization, manufacturing molds based on 3D design utilization.

WOORI M-Tech Co., Ltd. promises to strive to meet our clients' requirements based on honesty and sincerity.
